# AgentCollect — Brand Identity

## Name
**AgentCollect** (one word, camelCase: capital A, capital C)

## Tagline
**The end of manual collection.**

## Logo: "The Dot"
- Wordmark: `AgentCollect` in Plus Jakarta Sans 900 + **CIRCLE** dot (CSS `border-radius:50%`, NEVER a text period "." character)
- The dot is a CIRCLE — round, not square. This is a design element, not punctuation.
- Icon: Letter `A` (Plus Jakarta Sans 900) with circle dot top-right, on `#433196` background
- Primary color: `#433196` (deep purple, matches respaid.com CTA button)
- The dot = signature. It means: "Done. Resolved. Period."
- CSS implementation: `display:inline-block;width:7px;height:7px;border-radius:50%;background:#4F46E5`

## Positioning
- **What we do:** End manual debt collection — replaces internal teams, BPOs, and agencies with AI agents
- **Buyer:** CFO/CEO (mid-market) or VP AR (enterprise)
- **How it works:** AI agents call, email, negotiate under the CLIENT's brand
- **Key message:** "Build YOUR collection machine" (powered by AgentCollect)
- **Fear to address:** AI going rogue → logo/brand says "controlled, professional, under your brand"

## Usage Rules
- Never split "AgentCollect" into two colors — one weight or one color only
- The dot is ALWAYS indigo `#4F46E5` (or white on dark/indigo backgrounds)
- Icon works without the dot at small sizes (16px favicon)
- Light aesthetic preferred — avoid dark-heavy pages
- Same palette as respaid.com (continuity for existing clients)
