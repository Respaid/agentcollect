We hit an IVR wall.

The billing department phone tree promises help. Press 3 for billing, it says.

But pressing 3 doesn't connect you to anyone. It plays this message:

Salesforce IVR "Thank you for calling the Salesforce billing department. Please log in to our portal at billing dot salesforce dot com. Where you can get assistance from agent force, view your account, contract, and invoice details."
"You can also email us at billing at Salesforce dot com. Please be sure to include your account or contract number and a brief description of your request. Have a great day." Line disconnects.

No option to speak with someone. No callback list. No escalation path.

The system even failed to recognize basic inputs, repeating "Sorry. We did not recognize your response" multiple times before giving up entirely.

We called their collection agency.

Salesforce uses Verliance Inc for collections. Founded in 1999, based in Temecula, CA. Not BBB accredited.

But when we called, they answered as "Reliance Incorporated" — different name, same voicemail system.

Verliance/Reliance voicemail

Their business hours? Monday to Thursday, 7 AM to 3:30 PM Central. Friday, 7 AM to noon.

That's 29.5 hours per week — when most businesses operate 40+ hours.
