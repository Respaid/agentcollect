Company: HighRadius (the one from your screenshot). Pick your favorite layout.

Steve 🧠: The dossier is not the product — it's the hook. Its job is to make the prospect think "they know my business" in 5 seconds, then move to the voice demo. More info = more friction. Cut to what creates the "oh shit" moment: disputes they'll recognize + their current agency's rating (if applicable).

Current. Products + Payment Risk + Tags. Too much generic text. "Payment Risk Analysis" sounds clinical. "Professional services" is vague. The prospect doesn't recognize themselves here — it could be any company.

Focused. Only 2 dispute lines (industry-specific, feels real). Agency card shows either "DLL Finance — 2.1★ BBB" (Konica scenario) or "No external agency — absorbing losses" (most SaaS). This is the "how did they know that?" moment. Products section removed — the industry label already says enough.

Everything in one card. Single bordered card with icon-led lines. No section labels, no dots — just 4 lines of intel. Fastest to scan, least visual weight. Good for mobile. But loses the "dossier" feel that builds credibility.
