CONFIDENTIAL — IWS Revenue Recovery Assessment RPT-2026-0403-IWS
Detailed Findings
1. Identity Verification Gap High Impact
  • Call 1: Representative located a customer account by company name alone. Disclosed $34,818 balance, AP contact name, payment history, and email address without requesting any verification.
  • Call 2: Representative located a second account by name. Disclosed $13,305 balance, equipment details, service schedule, and multiple contact names.
  • No account number, callback verification, or security question was required on any call.
2. No Payment Collection Capability High Impact
  • No payment link, phone payment, or card-on-file option was offered on any of the three calls.
  • Two callers were ready to discuss their balance — neither was offered a way to pay.
  • Callers were directed to "use MyIWS.com" (offline) or "try BuildTrust" (unknown third-party).
3. Dispute Resolution Gap Medium Impact
  • A caller raised a $5,000 billing dispute related to a missed dumpster pickup.
  • The representative confirmed account details without verification.
  • Resolution offered: "Call your sales rep." No credit, no escalation path, no follow-up timeline provided.
4. Customer Portal Non-Functional High Impact
  • myiws.com → Domain listed for sale on HugeDomains ($2,695)
  • interstatewaste.com/myiws → 404 Not Found
  • interstatewaste.com/make-a-payment → 404 Not Found
  • interstatewaste.com/customer-portal → 404 Not Found
  • The IVR phone system actively directs customers to these non-functional URLs.
