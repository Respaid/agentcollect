Detailed Findings
1. No Identity Verification
0 / 10
  • Charlene looked up a CityMD account using only a company name and address. No account number, no callback verification, no security questions.
  • Disclosed: $34,818 outstanding balance, AP contact email, payment history, and primary contact name.
  • A competitor, vendor, or bad actor could extract the same data in under 2 minutes.
2. Pattern Confirmed: Second Rep, Same Result
0 / 10
  • Sue looked up Chelsea Piers by name alone. No verification attempted at any point during the call.
  • Disclosed: $13,305 balance, equipment inventory details, pickup schedule, and multiple contact names.
  • This is not an individual error. It is a systemic gap: no verification protocol exists.
3. Dispute Resolution Non-Existent
0 / 10
  • Nancy confirmed account details for a caller claiming a billing dispute, again without any form of verification.
  • Resolution offered: "Call your sales rep David." No case number, no escalation path, no follow-up committed.
  • Customer left with zero resolution. High risk of churn and delayed payment.
4. Customer Portal Broken
Critical
  • IWS phone IVR directs customers to MyIWS.com to make payments. That domain is currently for sale ($2,695).
  • Tested: /make-a-payment, /customer-portal, and /myiws all return 404 errors.
  • Customers who want to pay online cannot. Every payment requires a phone call that collects $0.
